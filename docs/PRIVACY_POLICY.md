# Privacy Policy — Hamster Run

_Last updated: 17th July 2026_

**Hamster Run** ("the App") is developed by Gogenius Dev. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

## Data We Collect
Hamster Run does **not** collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

The only data stored is your **high score**, which is saved locally on your device using `SharedPreferences`. This data:
- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to us or any third party
- Is deleted automatically if you uninstall the App

## Third-Party Services
The App does not use analytics, ads, or third-party SDKs that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect any information from children, as no personal data is collected at all.

## Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
